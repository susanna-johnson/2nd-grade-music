OUR SONGS

Unit 1 Sept 14-Nov 13, 2020
In Unit 1, we learned to compose melodies with stepwise motion and jumps using our first five notes: D, E, F#, G & A.
Students composed one song a week for nine weeks, increasing in complexity. The final songs have 1 jump in the melody and 2 beat patterns in the drums. Each student chose the instruments and the tempo (speed) of their song.
We used the software Chrome Music Lab Song Maker to compose these melodies. Please enjoy our work!
